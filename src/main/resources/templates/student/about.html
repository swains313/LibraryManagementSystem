About Library Management System

The Library Management System is an online platform for managing books, borrowers, and transactions in a library. It provides librarians with an easy way to keep track of their inventory, loan books to borrowers, and manage overdue fines. Borrowers can also use the system to search for books, reserve them, and view their borrowing history.
